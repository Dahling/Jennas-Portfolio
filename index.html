    • ABOUT ME

    Hello, dahling! My name is Jenna Christine Cooper. A psychiatrist once told me that I was pretty "dreamy." After looking at the synonyms of the word, I realized she was entirely correct; and, this remains true. After graduating with a degree in psychology and a minor in journalism, I pursued acting, thereafter graduating from a prestigious two-year acting school. Throughout my education, the plethora of jobs I've had, and all of life's adventures (thus far), I have always loved being on my computer. Whether I am writing, Googling my heart's desire, or fiddling around with a blog or website, one thing is certain: I love the process. And, at the end of the day, that's what led me to Epicodus: my true love for learning, creating, and playing the keys -the keys on my MacBook.
